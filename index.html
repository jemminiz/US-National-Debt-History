Welcome to The United States' National Fiscal History

A timeline of the finances of the United States

First: What is national debt?

According to the Merriam-Webster Dictionary, national debt is the total amount of money that the government of a country owes to companies, countries, etc.

Next: What is the scope of this project?

As this a project for NVCC's History 122 course, I will mainly be focusing on the financial history after 1865. However, I will give basic background to show overall growth. There will be more detail on the more modern era.
